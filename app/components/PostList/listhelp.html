Example Post:
Most parts of a post can also be hovered over for an explanation. Clicking on the bar itself will show the contents of the post.

Opening Time

This is the time (in the selected timezone) that the match opens.

Clicking on the calendar will make it turn red and (if notifications are enabled) you will get notifications when the match opening time hits the levels chosen in the settings. Default notification time is 10 minutes only.

Post title, team types and gamemodes

This is the title of the post. Clicking it will open the match post in a new window. The bolded text on the right is the team type of the game. The text underneath the title is the scenarios/gamemodes to be played with

Server Region

This is the region the server is hosted in

Match Host

This is the reddit name of the match host. Clicking it will open their user profile.

Clicking the Reddit alien will add the host to your 'favourite' list and their matches will show up green in the post list.

Server Address

This is the address to connect to to play the game. Clicking it will cause the IP to be copied to the clipboard (not on this example)
